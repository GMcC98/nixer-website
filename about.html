About Nixer

Nixer is an independent platform exploring global events and geopolitics through a fresh lens — combining AI-assisted research with curiosity-driven analysis. Each week, we dive into a trending topic, unpack it, and offer a grounded take designed to inform, challenge, and inspire.

The world is complex, but understanding it doesn’t have to be. Whether it’s trade wars, political shifts, or global conflicts, Nixer aims to make sense of it all — one post at a time.

This is a learning journey powered by generative AI, built to help others stay sharp in a fast-moving world. We’re not here to tell you what to think — just to help you think it through.
