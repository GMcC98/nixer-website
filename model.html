Our Model

Data Source & Methodology

Our risk model is underpinned by data from the Corruption Perceptions Index (CPI) 2024 by Transparency International.

Transparency International gathers data from expert assessments and opinion surveys to evaluate the perceived level of public sector corruption in over 180 countries. The CPI scores range from 0 (highly corrupt) to 100 (very clean). Our model leverages this data to assess risk in each country.

Data Source Description Methodology
Transparency International CPI 2024 Combines expert assessments and opinion surveys to produce a score reflecting perceived public sector corruption.
